# **App Name**: SignCraft Showcase

## Core Features:

- Product Slideshow: Display high-resolution SVG images of company products in a rotating slideshow.
- Contact Information: Display contact details including phone number, email address, and physical address.
- About Us Section: Provide a brief introduction to the company, highlighting its mission and values, including a placeholder for the company logo and relevant photos.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to evoke trust and professionalism, reflecting the established nature of the business.
- Background color: Light blue (#E8EAF6), subtly desaturated, maintaining a professional and clean aesthetic.
- Accent color: Purple (#7E57C2) to add a touch of sophistication and modernity to the interface.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif for headings, lending a sophisticated, high-end feel; body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for body text, for easy readability.
- Use minimalist icons related to company services.
- Clean and modern layout with a focus on visual hierarchy.
- Subtle transitions for a smooth user experience.
